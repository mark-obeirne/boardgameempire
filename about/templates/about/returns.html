Returns Policy

If you change your mind for any reason, we are happy to accept returns within 30 days of your order being shipped. Please contact us in advance, quoting your order number to ensure that your refund will be processed without delay.

To return an item, simply include the dispatch letter in the package and attach the returns label to the front of the package. Please note that items must be returned in a resellable condition and in their original packaging.

Damaged or defective items

While we take every care to ensure that our products arrive in perfect condition, accidents can happen. If you find that your items are faulty, incorrect, are missing components or parts, or have been damaged in transit, please contact us immediately and we will arrange for these items to be collected.

Please contact us within seven days of the date of delivery, including photos where possible.
